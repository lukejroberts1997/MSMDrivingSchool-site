Welcome to MSM Chesterfield Driving School. If you are interested in booking lessons or finding out what services we offer please click here to contact us. To ensure that all of our learner drivers find the process fun and memorable, our driving instructors offer a refreshing approach to driving tuition that will make you feel relaxed and eager to get on the road! All of our cars are brand new and have dual controls so you have complete peace of mind as you learn to drive with us.
As much as driving lessons can be a daunting experience, they are a special one too. From the moment you first get into the car and discover newfound confidence, to loving driving and passing your test, you can almost taste the freedom and opportunities ahead of you. With a flexible approach to teaching you how to drive we fit in around your busy schedule to help you pass first time. We teach you to drive, not just how to pass your test, so you can go out onto the road confident and assured in your own ability.
